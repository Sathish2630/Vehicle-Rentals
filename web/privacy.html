Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. At GoWheels, we are committed to protecting your personal information.

Introduction

GoWheels operates in the bike rental industry and values the trust you place in us when sharing your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What Information We Collect

  • Personal Identification: Name, email, phone number, address.
  • Rental Details: Bike type, rental duration, and location preferences.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, transaction history.

How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used for:

  • Processing and managing your bookings.
  • Improving our services and customer experience.
  • Sending updates and promotional offers (with your consent).

How We Protect Your Information

GoWheels uses industry-standard security measures to ensure your data is safe. We use encryption for sensitive data and regularly update our systems to prevent unauthorized access.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we have about you.
  • Request corrections or deletions of your data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@gowheels.com.
